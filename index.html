About Us

What makes our coffee special?

Coffee’s story begins centuries ago in the ancient coffee forests of Ethiopia. Legend has it that a goat herder named Kaldi discovered coffee when he noticed his goats became energetic after eating berries from a certain tree. This discovery sparked a journey that took coffee from Africa to the Middle East, Europe, and eventually the rest of the world. Today, coffee is one of the most beloved beverages, connecting people across cultures with its rich aroma and invigorating taste.

Over time, coffee has evolved into a cultural phenomenon. From the bustling cafés of Paris to the cozy coffee shops in every corner of the world, coffee has become more than just a drink. It has transformed into an experience—one that brings people together, sparks creativity, and fuels conversations. Whether it’s a cup shared between friends or a solo moment of reflection, coffee continues to be a source of comfort and inspiration.
